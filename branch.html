WELCOME TO ELECTRONICS AND TELECOMMUNICATION DEPARTMENT

About us :- The department of Electronics & Tele-Communication Engineering started in the year 2008 with the intake of 60 seats. Department is approved by All India Council for Technical Education New Delhi and is affiliated to Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education Mumbai. The Department has well Qualified Faculty members and well Equipped Laboratories to impart the required technical core skills and soft skills in students. Electronics & Tele-Communication Engineering offers scope in the field of Embedded System-a combination of hardware and software, mobile communication, Microwave & Radar communication, Radio communication, Consumer Electronics Fiber optics network, Satellite communication and computer networking. During the three years of Electronics & Tele-Communication Engineering diploma program, students attain industry /employer expected outcome in various courses. Student gain psychomotor skills of both the hardware and the software aspects. Hence the most important benefits that Electronics & Tele-Communication Engineering programprovides students is the freedom to excel between hardware field and software field. The students are motivated to participate in co-curricular as well extra-curricular activities for overall grooming of students. We organize seminars, workshops, industrial visits, guest lecture, sports and social so that students will become well versed with current technological development and soft skills with good citizen characters. To be a successful engineer, one has to be constantly updated with the emerging trends and technologies. So we focus on, learn to learn and lifelong learning process.
